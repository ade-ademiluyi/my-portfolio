First time i entered a plane


Growing up, i had always wondered what it felt like to be in a plane
flying from one place to another. Actually, it didn't even matter if the plane
was moving or not i just wanted to know what that felt like. But, that never really happened.
My parents didn't have that kind of money for me to have a feeling like that.
Still, i continued to wonder what it felt like.


Eventually, after 18 years of my life, i finally entered a plane and knew what
it felt like not because my parents finally had the money. but because i never
stopped wondering and i must say it felt amazing seeing the houses that would usually
look so big look so tiny that i could pick them
up with my fingers
