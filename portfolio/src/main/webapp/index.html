    First time i entered a plane


    Growing up, i had always wondered what it felt like to be in a plane
    flying from one place to another. Actually, it didn't even matter if the plane
    was moving or not i just wanted to know what that felt like. But,
    that never really happened. My parents didn't have that kind of money
    for me to have a feeling like that. Still, i continued to
    wonder what it felt like.


    Eventually, after 18 years of my life, i finally entered a plane and knew what
    it felt like not because my parents finally had the money. but because i never
    stopped wondering and i must say it felt amazing seeing those houses that would usually
    look so big look so tiny that i could pick them
    up with my fingers. Now you might be wondering how i was able to make that
    happen. My parents couldn't afford that then how you might ask? well, I promise you it
    didn't happen in my dream. it just happened the first time i entered a plane
    was the first time i was leaving my country to school in the united states
    on a scholarship. It only happened because i never stopped dreaming.

    Ade
